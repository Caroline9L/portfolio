Costume creation for theatre, magic, and cosplay

Private Clients

It may seem odd that my first focus is on private projects, but I list them at the top because they are my favorites. Shows have constraints to follow, but someone who wants a costume simply wants to be able to live their dream with no boundaries. Here you can see some of my favorite examples of making dreams come true through costumes that are immaculate in detail and perfectly fit.

Theatre

Currently I work as the Head Draper and workroom supervisor for the state theatre of New Jersey, Paper Mill Playhouse. This is one of my most exciting jobs to date because the organization is on the cutting edge -- many of the productions that we work on continue on to Broadway (including The Bandstand). I'm also lucky enough to be working in the heart of Manhattan with some of the best designers currently working. Prior to my move to Paper Mill, I worked as a first hand and supervisor for Parsons-Meares, Ltd, a costume house in Queens that builds some of the weirdest, most awesome things you may ever see on stage. Though my work there, I was able to contribute to Hamilton, Gigi, and several iterations of Disney on Ice productions, as well as costumes for The Radio City Rockettes and The Lion King. Before shifting my focus to NYC, I worked for the Shakespeare Theatre of NJ, with some of the most talented people I know.

Other Notable Work and Services

My side work spans beyond building costumes for private individuals. I specialize in design and construction for magicians; I am lucky enough to have the knowledge of the art to be able to build props as well as gimick clothing for whatever purpose the magician desires - and those guys are a super creative bunch! On the more mundane side, I am an experienced stylist for print as well as real-life applications (ie, you'll leave the house looking great!), as well as doing extensive practical work for academic theatre and dance.

Available Services:

  • Costume design for theatre, magic, and private functions
  • Costume construction (freelance and overhire)
  • Sewing services for magicians (propmaking, clothing alteration/gimmicking, garment construction)
  • Styling (Personal and Commercial)
  • Personal Shopping
  • Tailoring
